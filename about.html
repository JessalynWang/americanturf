OUR MISSION

Welcome to the Murican’ Turf! Using our knowledge about the environment and a single square meter of land, we plan to grow the finest produce in the U.S. for half a year and then dip. During our season of labor, we will humbly donate our fresh herbs and vegetables to craft exquisite dishes and feed the starving juniors and seniors of AHS. The Murican’ Turf encourages a sustainable, healthy environment, as well as a healthy diet!

Search our website for the secrets to growing a successful garden!
